About Michael

A New Yorker since 1992, Michael specializes in luxury real estate sales throughout Manhattan, delivering extraordinary service to buyers, sellers and investors alike.

Michael brings a wide-ranging marketing background, charm and professionalism to his real estate career. With more than 17 years of experience in Manhattan's luxury real estate, Michael possesses the experience and knowledge necessary to help buyers and sellers make the most informed decisions regarding their real estate transactions.

Working alongside premiere Manhattan broker Lauren Muss since 2005 who is consistently recognized by the Wall Street Journal as one of the "Top 100" Agents in the nation and "Top 10" in New York City Michael delivers thorough service to a discerning clientele of buyers and sellers with a broad range of needs in transactions raging from coops, condos, townhouses and new development in all prime Manhattan neighborhoods.

His tremendous enthusiasm, friendly professionalism, technological know-how and exacting attention to detail, combined with the thorough knowledge of the market that his more-than-30-years living in New York City afford him, make Michael a valuable resource at your disposal as your real estate agent. He bases his success on the trust his clients derive from knowing he puts their interests first.

Michael lived in Rome, Madrid, and Miami before making New York City his home, and is fluent in Spanish and Italian. He holds a degree in International Trade & Marketing from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York. As a resident of the Upper West Side, Michael enjoys long walks with his dog, admiring the historical charm and architectural distinctiveness of the neighborhood. In his spare time, Michael enjoys painting contemporary artworks, which have been exhibited in local shows and internationally.
